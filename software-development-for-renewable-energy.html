Why Renewable Energy is so important?

Renewable energy is becoming increasingly vital and is the key to a more sustainable future of the energy industry. The opportunities it presents are enormous, as according to Bloomberg there is a need of $1.3 Trillion per Year of investments by 2030 to achieve Climate Goals.

Green energy is a key element for building overall solution to energetic independence and security of EU* and its countries. Energy crisis caused by a number of external factors clearly proved that depending on fossil fuels is not viable long term.

What kind of challenges have we spotted?

A central challenge is the dependence of energy generation on local weather conditions (sun or wind). This makes it difficult to plan, which has an impact on efficiency and costs

During number of interactions within professional network we have observed increasing need in:

providing better oversight on operations;

improving energy consumption predictions;

preventing unexpected downtimes;

improving maintenance transparency.
