About us

Iterative Engineering is a software house dedicated to provide reliable, high quality and efficient software for business and industry.

Our biggest asset is professional team and dedicated individuals being part of it. All of them are experienced engineers eager to take on challenging tasks and capable to provide best possible solutions.

As a company we have years of experience in IT projects for such industries as Oil&Gas, Health&Lifestyle and e-Commerce. Providing our clients with expertise and comprehensive software development services focused around web and mobile software development.

We believe that software we build should be driven by and for business and while working for our clients we take extra care to ensure such. Therefore we utilize agile process and follow iterative approach, frequently reviewing and consulting our activities with a client to provide maximum transparency. This ensures that customer receives a high quality product that brings required value into his business at requisite time.
