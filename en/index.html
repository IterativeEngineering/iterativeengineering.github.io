SELECTED PROJECTS

GO2 TRAVEL

The application allows for a quick quote and purchase of travel insurance - the whole process takes a few minutes, and the purchased insurance is activated within one hour of receiving the confirmation.

As part of the application, the user can choose the direction of travel, the date of departure and the length of stay as well as precisely define the scope of insurance, taking into account various standards and a wide range of additional options. In the final stage, the user receives an insurance quote in accordance with the previously indicated preferences and has the option to purchase and pay for this insurance directly through the application.

In addition, the user has full access to the history of current and past insurance covers (including all documents and regulations), and the chat function allowing for direct contact with consultants with any questions or claims.

ADSCORE

Website and client panel for AdScore.com

Adscore is a global, advertising verification provider specializing in improving the effectiveness of marketing campaigns, enabling the analysis of the quality of traffic redirected by individual advertisers to a selected website. It verifies whether "clicks" and redirects are the result of human and potential customer activity, or if they are the result of bots, unfair competition or spammers.

The main goal of the project is to increase transparency among providers from the advertising industry and, as a result, reduce or even completely eliminate fraud traffic from ads.

Importantly, AdScore is an independent tool, that allows for objective and reliable data verification. It gives users the opportunity to compare peroformance of selected advertisers while also providing easy way to create claim reports about fraudulent traffic.

WAKACJE.IN

The application gives users access to the database of all tours operated by the majority of Polish TourOperators.

It gives user the opportunity to search for the most attractive offers from a given region (both in Poland and around the world) and to book and purchase the selected offer. The entire process, from finding a trip to purchasing it, is fully digitized, therefore it runs very smoothly and does not require the support of consultants.
