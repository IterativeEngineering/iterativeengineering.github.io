Who we are?

Iterative Engineering is a software house dedicated to providing high quality, modern and reliable software for business and industry.

Our biggest asset is the team. Its smart and dedicated engineers can handle any challenging task. Their skills, knowledge and experience enable us to provide comprehensive software planning, design, development and implementation services.

We believe in a "Software driven by Business"​ principle and we perform projects using iterative approach, constantly reviewing and consulting our activities with a client. This maximizes results of our work, and ensures that customer receives a high quality product that brings value into his or her business.

As a team we have many years of experience in IT projects for such as industries as oil&gas, IT and commercial sectors. In addition to our main software development activity, we also provide training and consulting services in the field of IT.
