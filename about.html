ITERATIVE ENGINEERING

Our unique strength is a combination of proficiency in software development with a great understanding of engineering domains and their specific language. We can build meaningful solutions quicker, avoid unnecessary discussions and provide sensible suggestions on how to maximize business value.

VALUES THAT WE SHARE

PASSION

In our case, this is synonymous with work. We are inspired by our clients' needs and the challenges they face, while modern technologies and programming drive us to action and encourage continuous development. We feel fully responsible for what we do, therefore we put maximum commitment in our work.

EFFICIENCY

"Time is money" - is one of the slogans that we follow in the implementation of all projects. We act in such a way as to maximise effects and build value as quickly as possible, therefore we always advise and select solutions adequate to a given situation. Thanks to this we minimise unnecessary burdens and thus reduce our clients' expenses.

PROFESSIONALISM

We are engineers. We are concrete and substantive. We use proven methods and extensive experience. We are also open to new solutions, flexible and contactable. We are constantly developing and all the specialists who make up our team are independent and efficient.

CREATIVITY

While standard methods work well for 80% of tasks, the remaining 20% often require non-standard measures, which our team copes with equally well. We are able to creatively approach new challenges and analyse them from different perspectives.

TEAMWORK

We are a close-knit team that trusts each other, which allows us to work effectively and efficiently. Together we face the toughest challenges, we know each other's strengths and weaknesses, so we can rely on each other and support each other in completing tasks.

PROACTIVITY

LOCATION

Even though the language of IT is international and the virtual space has no borders, we chose the Metropoly of Silesia as our place to live and work in.

It is one of the biggest technological hubs in Poland. Apart from Iterative Engineerg, companies such as Rockwell, PubNub and IBM have their headquarters here.

GMT+1 Timezone.

Cultural proximity to other European countries and a very high level of foreign language skills, especially English and German.

Scientific and educational facilities in the form of renowned technical colleges and universities.

Developed technical and communication infrastructure - three international airports within a 100km radius, a well-developed network of roads and motorways and a modern ICT infrastructure.
