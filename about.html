ABOUT US

INSPIRED BY YOUR NEEDS

We are a modern and rapidly developing software house that develops software for the business and industrial sectors. Thanks to our knowledge and experience, we offer our clients a comprehensive service - from analysis and design, through implementation and testing of solutions, to their deployment and ongoing maintenance.

We are also an excellent partner for technically conscious companies, as we are able to immediately implement the project and provide the necessary modern and functional solutions exactly where they are needed. We also feel at home with customers for whom technology is a complex concept. Our cooperation is based on knowledge and trust, because we are able to talk about digitisation and IT in an accessible language and translate business requirements into tailored and effective technological actions.

Our greatest asset is a close-knit and professional team of specialists, thanks to whom we can act in such a versatile and universal way. It is the combination of engineering knowledge, soft skills and business thinking that is our hallmark and makes us not afraid of any challenges.

LOCATION

Even though the language of IT is international and the virtual space has no borders, we chose the Metropoly of Silesia as our place to live and work in.

  • It is one of the biggest technological hubs in Poland and home to companies like Rockwell Automation, PubNub, IBM and Iterative Engineering!
  • GMT+1 Timezone.
  • Cultural proximity to other European countries and a very high level of foreign language skills, especially English and German.
  • Scientific and educational facilities in the form of renowned technical colleges and universities.
  • Developed technical and communication infrastructure - three international airports within a 100km radius, a well-developed network of roads and motorways and a modern ICT infrastructure.
