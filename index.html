SELECTED PROJECT

GO2 TRAVEL

The application allows for a quick quote and purchase of travel insurance - the whole process takes a few minutes, and the purchased insurance is activated within one hour of receiving the confirmation.

The user selects the direction of travel, the date of departure and the length of stay, and determines the scope of insurance, taking into account various standards and a wide range of additional options. In the final stage, they receive an offer in line with the indicated preferences and have the option to purchase and pay for insurance directly through the application.

In addition, the user has full access to the history of his insurance covers, and the chat function allowing for direct contact with consultants with any questions or claims.

ADSCORE

Website and client panel for AdScore.com.

Adscore is a tool that improves the effectiveness of marketing campaigns and enables the analysis of the quality of traffic redirected by individual advertisers to the selected website. It checks whether "clicks" are caused by human activity and a potential customer, or whether they are the result of bots, unfair competition or spammers.

AdScore is an independent tool, that allows for objective data verification, and the main purpose of the application is to increase transparency among suppliers from the advertising industry and, as a result, eliminate fraudulent traffic from ads.

ADTECH

PRIMO NUTRITION

An application designed for people who care about a healthy and optimal diet, used to track and monitor the amount of vitamins and micro- and macroelements consumed.

Initially, the application allows the user to determine the nutritional requirements based on the characteristics of the lifestyle, health and general condition.

Then, the user can enter information about the meals consumed during the day (manually or by scanning the product packaging), and the application analyzes their content and presents a report on the amount of nutrients consumed and suggests what elements should be supplemented in the diet to make it balanced and adapted to needs.

MOBILE
LIFESTYLE

HEALTHLY

Healthly is an application that gives medical specialists and their potential patients the opportunity to make appointments in the form of videoconsultations.

It is a "virtual clinic" available to everyone, which brings together experts from many medical specialties and allows patients to conveniently and quickly contact specialists from all over the country - without leaving home.

When using Healthly, experts receive a payment guarantee, and patients are only serviced by verified and reliable professionals. In addition, thanks to the encrypted connection, both the personal data of specialists and their patients are completely safe.

WAKACJE.IN

The Wakacje.in application gives users access to the database and search engine of tours operated by the majority of Polish Tour Operators.

It allows the user to easily find the most advantageous offers from a given region (both in Poland and around the world) and gives the opportunity to book and finally purchase the selected offer.

The entire process, from finding a trip to purchasing it, is fully digitized, therefore it runs very smoothly and does not require the support of consultants.

CARBON INTENSITY

Desktop application for assessing CO2 emissions from production areas, fields, oil rigs and individual shareholders involved in the oil and gas industry.

The application is complex and highly technical, with extensive functionality and a large amount of specialist data presented. It also takes into account different perspectives on data analysis.
